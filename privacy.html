Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

1. Introduction

The **Cedar Grove Homeowners Association (HOA) Website** is primarily an informational site for our community. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our adherence to this policy.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

The Cedar Grove HOA Website **does not directly collect any personal identifying information** from visitors. Specifically:

  • We **do not** use login accounts.
  • We **do not** use contact forms that submit data to a server.
  • We **do not** collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or residential addresses from general website visitors.

3. Third-Party Data (e.g., Website Hosting)

Our Website is hosted using third-party services (GitHub Pages). These services may automatically collect standard non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit. This data is used solely to **monitor website performance and traffic** and is not linked to any specific individual.

4. External Links

This Website contains links to third-party sites (e.g., businesses, government resources). Once you click on these links, we have no control over the privacy practices of those external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the HOA Board using the email address and mailing address provided on the main page.
